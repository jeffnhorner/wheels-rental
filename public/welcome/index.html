Frequently asked questions

When does the rental period begin? When am I charged?

The rental period begins the day the bike is delivered or picked up.

How long will it take to receive my Wheels bike?

If you opt for delivery, it may take 2-3 business days to fulfill your order. However, we do offer pick up at our service center for faster fulfillment.

Is there a security deposit?

Wheels will charge you a $99 security deposit. It will be refunded when your bike is returned.

Can two people ride on a Wheels?

No, only one person per Wheels bike!

Where am I allowed to ride?

Please do not ride your bike on sidewalks. They are meant for the road or bike lanes.

Am I required to wear a helmet?

Yes please! Safety first!

Where am I allowed to park?

Please park by locking your bike to a designated bike rack. We do not recommend leaving your Wheels out for an extended period of time.

How long does it take to charge my bike?

Approximately 3 hours

Can I store my Wheels outside?

Do not store your Wheels outside. It’s small, compact & only weighs 45 lbs.

What happens if my bike is stolen?

You accept responsibility for your bike. If your bike is stolen, we will help you locate the bike with our GPS but it is your responsibility to recover the vehicle otherwise you may be charged for the value of the device.

Can I purchase my Wheels?

Not yet! We’re currently working on a plan that will allow you to purchase your Wheels.
